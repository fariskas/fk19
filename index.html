FARIS KASSIM
MENU & FILTERS
I’m Faris Kassim, a visual artist, interactive designer and a creative coder currently based between Seoul and Singapore. I conceptualise and develop exploratory non-linear websites, digital art installations and live generative code-based visuals.

I have worked with various brands and personalites such as Adidas, Prada, Red Bull, IVY PARK (Beyoncé), Humanrace (Pharrell Williams) and SM Entertainment, cultural institutions such as Arts Council of Korea (ARKO), Singapore Art Museum, National Gallery of Singapore.

Currently a part of OKOK Services, an independent creative studio I co-founded with My Kim Bui, having previously worked at studios and agencies such as Bravo, Asylum, Ogilvy and Plus X – helping brands and start-ups establish a strong digital presence online.

My work has been featured on various websites and publications such as The FWA, Awwwards, It's Nice That, The Brand Identity, IdN Magazine, HTTPSTER, Hover States, HalloInternet, Brutalist Websites.

If you have interesting collaborations and commissions in mind, drop me an email at areyou@okok.services

CV and more of my past and current works are available on request.
