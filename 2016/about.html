I’m Faris Kassim, a freelance interactive designer and a front-end developer currently based in Singapore and Seoul. I specialise in designing and developing websites, apps and digital art installations.

My work has been featured on various websites and publications such as The FWA, Awwwards, IdN Magazine, CSS Design Awards, HTTPSTER, DesignAwards Asia, Brutalist Websites.

Previously worked at studios and agencies such as Bravo, Asylum and Ogilvy, helping brands and start-ups establish a strong digital presence online.

Say hi@fariskassim.com if you have an interesting collaboration in mind.

CV and more of my past works and more current are available on request.
